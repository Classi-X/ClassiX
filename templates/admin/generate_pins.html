Understanding PINs

What are PINs?
PINs (Personal Identification Numbers) are secure codes generated by the admin to allow new users to register.

Who should use them?
Students or teachers who want to sign up must enter a valid PIN provided by the admin.

Admin Instructions:

  • Select the role (student/teacher) for whom you are generating the PINs.
  • Share the generated PINs with users securely. Avoid sharing in public groups.
  • Once used, the PIN becomes invalid.

Tip: Keep a record of which PIN was shared with whom to avoid confusion.
