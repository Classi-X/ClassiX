QR Code Attendance Instructions
For Students
  • Click "Start Scanner" to enable your device’s camera.
  • When asked, allow camera access in your browser/device settings.
  • Hold the QR code (provided by your teacher) in front of your camera within the frame.
  • Ensure the area is well-lit and keep the camera steady for quick scanning.
  • Once scanned, your attendance will be marked automatically.
Manual Token Entry
  • If QR scan fails or your camera doesn't work, ask your teacher for the token.
  • Enter the token exactly as provided – it is case-sensitive.
  • Tokens usually expire within 15 minutes, so enter them promptly.
  • Click "Mark Attendance" to submit the form.
Tip: If the scanner isn't working, try refreshing the page or switching to a different browser (e.g. Chrome or Firefox).
